Class: XmmProcessor

XmmProcessor

Representation of a gesture model. A instance of XmmPorcessor can train a model from examples and can perform classification and/or regression depending on the chosen algorithm.

The training is currently based on the presence of a remote server-side API, that must be able to process rapidMix compliant JSON formats.

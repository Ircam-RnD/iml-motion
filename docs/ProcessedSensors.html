Class: ProcessedSensors

ProcessedSensors

High-level abstraction that listen for raw sensors (accelerometers and gyroscpes) and apply a bunch of preprocessing / filtering on it.

output :

  • IntensityNorm
  • IntensityX
  • IntensityY
  • IntensityZ
  • IntensityNormBoost
  • BandPass AccX
  • BandPass AccY
  • BandPass AccZ
  • Orientation X
  • Orientation Y
  • Orientation Z
